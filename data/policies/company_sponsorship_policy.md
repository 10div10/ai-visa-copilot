# Company Internal Policy — Visa Sponsorship (Internal FAQ)

## Who is eligible for sponsorship?
Full-time employees who have completed 6 months of employment are eligible for H1-B sponsorship. Contractors and interns are not eligible under this policy.

## What does the company provide?
- Immigration attorney fees for petition filing (I-129) are covered by the company.
- Employment Verification Letter is issued by HR within 5 business days of request via the HR portal ticket system.
- The company does NOT cover dependent (H4) visa fees; employees must self-fund dependent applications.

## Timeline
- Petition filing typically takes 4-6 weeks from LCA certification.
- Premium processing (15 calendar days) is available at employee's request but employee bears the USCIS premium processing fee.

## Internal Escalation
If an employee's I-797 has not arrived within 90 days of filing, they should raise a ticket with the Global Mobility team, not general HR.
